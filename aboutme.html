About Me

Hi! Welcome to my website. A little about me.

The purpose of this website is the document a journey of a lifetime! I am a kid with BIG aspirations. I have realized that life is short, so enjoy it!

I was able to develop a business mentally in high school through my robotics program. However, my focus on the business aspect of the team paused my opportunity to create something that gave me joy deep inside. My love for building LEGOs to developing short films and working in UT's TexasInventionWorks ignited my love to innovate. My favorite thing to do is to do the unexpected and make the impossible as possible and change the world by pushing my boundaries to grows. The website is the documentation of my journey to follow my dreams and change the world one step at a time. The website started winter break of my 2nd year in college after taking my first coding class, I wanted to learn more so here I am!

My hope is for you to enjoy it as much as I am making it! :)
